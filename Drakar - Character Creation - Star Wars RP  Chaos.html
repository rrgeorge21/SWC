[Personality]

Ruthless. Calm. Manipulative.

 

Drakar can be called all of these, but it is only a small image of his true being. Once a custodian of the Light Side of the Force, Drakar's quest for power unhindered by the machinations of others drove him into the welcoming arms of the Dark Side. As a high-ranking member of the Imperial Knights, he came dangerously close to being stripped of his abilities and cast out due to his work in experimenting with Sith alchemy. Uninterested in allowing this to happen again, Drakar turned the entire Empire against the Knights by revealing their affiliation with the Rebellion, personally slaying some of his closest colleagues during the resulting purge.

 

As an Inquisitor, he is given more autonomy to be...free. No longer burdened by the philosophies of morality, Drakar's directive has been to seek out any artefacts or knowledge that would strengthen him and others that wish to follow him on the path to power. His mission is further spurred on by his anger at not becoming the next grand inquisitor after the disappearance of Lord Morcus, instead, he was passed up by someone of lesser power than him. However, this galaxy-wide search has not deafened him to present events, especially those concerning a dark crusade against a long-lived plague: the Jedi. 

 

In preparation for war, Drakar has moulded himself into a capable military commander, unafraid of leading armies into the fray or ensuring the victory of entire fleets. His massive arsenal of Force powers makes him a valuable ally or a deadly enemy.

[Appearance]

Looks can be deceiving when it comes to Drakar. On the exterior, he may look the part of a fifty-year-old man, nearly bald and blinded by an injury from his youth, standing six feet tall and weighing only a few dozen kilograms, but inside the man is so much more. While he has entered the late stages of middle-age, Drakar is surprisingly agile and full of stamina, able to commit to Force-infused feats of strength and speed.

 

Playing the part of an Inquisitor perfectly, Drakar dons midnight black robes with red highlights when carrying out his duties and supplements this with durable armour when he enters battle, knowing that even though the Force can protect him, it doesn't hurt to be practical. Along with this, he wears a thin strap of leather to cover his eyes, acknowledging that the organs were rendered useless after suffering an accident involving electricity.

 

Clipped to his side is a standard lightsaber that, when activated, sports a crimson blade. Due to the possibility of being unarmed, he also has a pair of crushgaunts fitted over his hands for close-quarters combat.
