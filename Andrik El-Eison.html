  • Fighter Pilot Heritage
    Like his parents, and their parents before them, and... oh, who really knows how far back it goes? Rik has been schooled in as much piloting as a kid could squeeze out of his parents, and if not them, their squadmates... or his grandfather. He has grown into a strong pilot, though splitting time between keeping those skills sharp, formal schooling, and Jedi training has been quite the challenge.

    Technical Aptitude
    For as long as he's had the strength to pull things apart, Rik has been taking things apart and putting them back together... or not. As a young child, he was known to pilfer parts from one thing to build another, much to the considerable concern of his parents when at times the thing being taken from was the family droid, and the thing being given to was a bomb. Explosions are cool, could ya blame him? The years have tempered this part of him into something more considerate and/or more useful, though the odd prank can be too difficult to resist, like turning the temple lighting into a disco, for example... anyone?
