Name: Adam Jensen
Origin: Deus Ex
Gender: Male
Classification: Mechanically augmented human
Age: 34
Powers and Abilities: Super strength, speed, dexterity, durability, endurance and stamina, master marksman, expert CQC/infiltrator, invisibility, regeneration (at least mid-low), extendable nanoceramic blades built into his arms, an inbuilt HUD system complete with a radar, slow time perception with increased reflexes, electromagnetic pulse waves and fileds augment his movement and jumpling/landing abilities, the ability to release pheromones in conversations to influence people, immunity to toxic gases and EMPs, can see through walls, advanced hacking skill due to a microcomputer implanted in his head
Weaknesses: Many of his advanced abilities require energy to use, and when this energy runs out he needs nutrients in order to fully recharge it, however we see in the comic in contrast to the game, that he can spam his abilities quite frequently and that it's not a real issue
Destructive Capacity: Wall level+ normally, building level with Typhoon and certain weapons
Range: Human melee range extended by guns and his blades, several meters with Typhoon
Speed: Hypersonic (Mach 6+)
Durability: At least small building level due to sub-dermal armor, can survive being at ground zero of an explosion of this scale
Strength: Superhuman+
Striking Strength: Class KJ (can destroy concrete/steel walls by punching through them)
Stamina: Peak human+ (never particularly shown to tire, most of Human Revolution took place over just a few days)
Standard Equipment: Wields numerous weapons over the course of the game, but mostly relies on what he scavenges from the battlefield, was shown to carry a combat rifle on him a the beginning of the game, posters should probably specify what guns, if any, he gets
Intelligence: A skilled and experienced strategist, very knowledgeable about electronics and augmentations in general with special training in detective work and stealth. A side effect of his augmentations has granted him an increased amount of intelligence and superhuman-level multitasking and learning capabilities.
Notable Attacks/Techniques:

- Typhoon : Launches a barrage of mini-explosives in a 360 degree arc, with a range of 8 meters. When fully upgraded it is powerful enough to destroy large mechs.
