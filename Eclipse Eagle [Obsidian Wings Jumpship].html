      STRENGTHS
      • Advanced Stealth System: The Specter Light Stealth Transport has a stealth system that, when engaged, lowers the ship's ambient energy emissions to near zero levels. This causes the ship's sensor echo to appear much smaller, like that of a small asteroid or piece of space debris.
      • Communication Jammers: The Eaagle has a powerful transmitter assembly that can broadcast a jamming signal for approximately 50,000 kilometers in each direction. This signal interrupts most basic communications tech in this area.
      • Adaptive Camouflage: The hull plating has been coated with pigment changing micro-projectors, which allows the crew to change the ship's color scheme to match their surroundings. For example, when flying in open space, the hull can be turned jet black with specs of white to simulate a normal space backdrop. If flying over or through a jungle, the hull could be turned green and brown to better blend in with the trees and native vegetation. This is not a true cloaking device and does not actually make the ship invisible, it just makes the ship more difficult to detect visually.
      WEAKNESSES
      • Maintenance Heavy: The Specter class light stealth transport utilizes advanced state-of-the-art stealth technology. As such, it requires a high level of maintenance to work optimally. If maintenance is neglected, then the ship's various systems will eventually begin to malfunction to the point of being inoperable.
      • Not a Beast: While it is fast and certainly maneuverable, the Eclipse Eagle is not some magic survive-all ship. If it gets hit by any capital grade weapons, like any other freighter, it's going to take some serious damage.
      • Very Expensive: Upgrading this already highly modified ship is extremely expensive. It will take thousands of credits for better parts.
      DESCRIPTION

      The Eagle is a OSA-06 Specter Light Stealth Transport that was purchased and then extensively modified by A'Sura Den. It maintains the high rate of speed and maneuverability as well as advanced stealth systems which limit its sensor echos, adaptive camouflage hull plating which make it difficult to spot visually and powerful communication jammers to confuse and otherwise limit the enemy's ability to coordinate counterstrikes against or intercepts of the craft, that was indicative of the original craft. In place of the section meant to house troops, A'Sura installed an armory/workshop and also added aftermarket weapons, upgraded the standard shield array and added a chaff/mine launcher to the rear of the craft.
