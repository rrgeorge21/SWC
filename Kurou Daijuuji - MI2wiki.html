Personality

Kurou has a strong sense of justice and morality, claiming that there is no rationale behind his desire to aid and protect others beyond the fact that it would leave him with lingering regrets if he simply abandoned them, a feeling that he describes as "a bad aftertaste".

Background

He was enrolled as an archaeology student at the Miskatonic University but used this as a cover for his true major in sorcery under the tutelage of Dr. Henry Armitage. During an attempt to break into the library to read one of the forbidden grimoires, he was attacked by Wilbur Whateley and almost killed, causing him to distance himself from the occult and drop out of the university entirely. He continued to work as a private investigator but was financially poor until the day he was hired by the Hadou Financial Group to acquire a grimoire. In his search, he encounters Al-Azif and circumstances force him into a contract with her, becoming the pilot of the imitation Deus Machina, Demonbane (デモンベインDemonbein?). After encountering the inhumanity of Master Therion, he comes to hate the man with a passion, fuelling his determination to destroy the Black Lodge.

Special Abilities

Magius Form: By fusing with Al-Azif, Kurou can attain this form, which grants him the following abilities: Super strength, durability and speed, agility, endurance, and magic. flight and physical barriers via Magius Wing, can transform Magius Wing into any forms that he imagines and use it like a part of his body, as well as binding movements and vision of the enemy via his pages, energy blasts, and also trap, grab and binding his enemy with his hair via Atlach-Nacha.
Combat Skills: Skilled swordman and gunslinger and Hand to Hand combatant.
Magic: Can enhance his Scimitar of Barzai with fire and create a sea of flame, resist against mental, spiritual attacks and can be enhanced with Elder Sign and barriers, can find weak point(s) of opposing spells to either dispel, hijack and control them, can accelerate the speed of his consciousness and sense in order to match with Deus Machina's speed. These abilities are GREATLY enhanced in Magius Form, but Kurou does not need Al-Azif to use them.

Powers & Merits

Pact With Al-Azif: Kurou is bound to the Necronomicon until his death. This has given him several unique powers, listed above. These are all powers that any master of the Necronomicon can use, but since the pact is life-long and intimately linked to Kurou, this is listed as a power.

Unique Items

Necronomicon: His Grimiore which takes the form of the girl Al-Azif, allows Kurou to transform into the Magius Style and summon Demonbane (disabled for this channel).
Atlach-Nacha: Allows Kurou's Magius Form to use his hair as binding weapons, also enhanced Demonbane with Spider-like powers and abilities.
The Scimitar of Barzai: Kurou's main hand to hand weapon. A blade that can cut through almost anything on a conceptual level (treat as unbelievably sharp and unbreakable). Kurou can throw it like a spinning blade of death and direct it to attack one or multiple opponents.
Mirror of Nitocris: Allows for Illusion Creation, invisibility and mirror coating.
Cthugha and Ithaqua: Dual-Wielded Handguns, bound to Kurou. Cthugha can fire blasts of unimaginable heat, and Ithaqua can fire bullets of ice far blow absolute zero. Both handguns' bullets will home onto an enemy's weak points and follow them even if they try to dodge.

Weaknesses & Flaws

Item-Dependent: Without the Necronomicon, Kurou cannot summon the Mirror of Nitocris, the Scimitar of Barzai, or use his Magius form, leaving him with only his basic magical knowledge, and Cthugha and Ithaqua. Obviously, without his handguns, he does not have guns. Most of Kurou's power is intimately dependent on Al-Azif, as without her, he is a mage of no small skill but not exceptional power.
Human: Kurou is exceptionally resilient, and he can heal himself in Magius Form. However, if attacked without the protection of Al-Azif, he is as vulnerable as any human being, which is only considered a weakness because most of the things and people he fights are capable of destroying cities, are alien abominations, or are super powerful robots.
Lazy: Kurou is very hard to motivate to do anything.
