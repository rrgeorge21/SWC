Defender-class Corvette



Ship Class: Corvette
Ship Role: Moblie Command Center/Corvette
Ship Length: 75 meters
Agility (A): 3
Defensive (D): 5
Offensive (O): 4
Speed (S): 3
Special Gear: 3 (Cloaking Device, High-Powered Sensor Array)

Weapons:
  • 2 Heavy Dual Turbolaser Cannons
  • 2 Quad Laser Cannons
  • 4 Dual Autoblasters point-defense turrets
  • 2 Heavy Swivel-mounted Ion Cannons
  • 4 AR 10 Launchers (24 torpedoes and 24 missiles)

Maximum Acceleration: 2,450 G
Maximum Atmospheric Speed: 1,050 KM/H
MGLT: 90
Hyperdrive Rating: Class 0.5
Back-up Hyperdrive: Class 14
Complement: 20 (1+Astromech minimum)

Description:

As the Aesir line began to round itself out with its variety of ships capable of different tasks, only a single thought remained. While the larger ships served as mobile bases or flagships, there existed no smaller ship that served as a better mobile platform for high ranking officials or small teams among the factions. Through debate as well as trial and error, the eventual plans came to shape the Defender-class Corvette. Overall, the design and setup look inspiration from the ancient Defender-class Corvette created by the Old Republic, though they sought to improve on a few features from speed, firepower, and stealth capabilities.

Creating a mix of power for the ship instead of just full-on capital ship combat, 2 dual heavy turbolaser cannons, and two Quad Laser Cannons fixed to turrents for frontal capital ship assault while the six dual autoblaster point-defense turrets defend it from starfighters, especially the rear pair that help protect its engines. Two heavy ion cannons fixed themselves on each side of the cockpit on swiveling mounts that can hit the front, top, or back, making it effective for running under larger ships and an unloading ion blasts into the shields. For a final touch, a set of AR 10 Launchers with variable magazines were affixed to the front of the ship.

Meant to be a mobile command ship meant for small teams or high ranking officials, the ship was designed to function with a minimal crew, needing no more than ten to fifteen people to operate the vessel. To move more freely, it was a quick ship with an incredibly fast hyperdrive as well as a stealth field generator. For boarding actions or ground assaults, it could carry over 20 troops.
