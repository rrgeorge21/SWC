BIOGRAPHY:

Born to a union of Corellian new money and Serennian nobility, Adrian was taught from an early age that he was destined for great things. That he was above the common rabble. Spending much of his early childhood travelling alongside his parents on their frequent business trips, he quickly developed an intense fondness for the written word, reading about the glories of the past to break up the monotony of travel. Then, as so often happens in this harsh galaxy, tragedy struck. With the devastation of Corellia, much of his paternal family was wiped out in one fell swoop, deeply traumatising the young boy. After this, the small family began spending much of their time on Serenno, where Adrian would eventually come to attend a prestigious academy for the exceptionally talented, and equally well connected, youth.

 

It was during this time that he first discovered his force sensitivity; spontaneously manifesting a force barrier in self-defence, then gunning down the assailing mugger in cold blood. Keeping this ability hidden from all but those closest to himself, he began a lengthy study of the Force and its secrets, assisted by the underworld contacts of his grandfather, the Baron Terassi. Already on a route to the dark side, he nevertheless stayed above the tipping point, but that all changed when his parents were kidnapped by a Hutt crime lord. Setting out to rescue them, his attempt ended in failure and would have likely cost him his life, or at the very least his freedom, had it not been for the timely intervention of a travelling Sith Knight. After having him commit acts so vile they would haunt his dreams for months, the Knight was convinced of his worth. Of his utter ruthlessness.

 

His parents were rescued and returned to Serenno, but he... he was taken to Bastion, to join the Sith, or die trying.

 

The trials were harsh. The Overseers ruthless. He learned, he adapted, he survived. From delving through trap-riddled tombs to surviving the wilds of Korriban for months on end, he overcame it all. Feral beasts. Prisoners destined for torture and execution. A fellow Acolyte or two. All died at his hands, victims of his burgeoning abilities. In the end, he was among the lucky ones. The ones that survived. The ones that might one day join the ranks of the true Sith.
