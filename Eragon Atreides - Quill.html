NAME: Quillian Kaohal ~ Australis

FACTION: Mandalorian Clan of Australis

RANK / TITLE:
—> Kishin’Narvada​
—> Alpha​

SPECIES: Gurlanin

AGE: Appears 13 / is actually 91 ( Every 7 Standard Years adds 1 year to his “appears” age )

SEX: Male

HEIGHT: 5’ ( 153 cm)

WEIGHT: 120 lbs ( 55 kg )

APPEARANCE: ( As a Human )

EYES: A fierce and vibrant Emerald​
HAIR: Unkempt - Spiked Ivory​
SKIN: Tanned, Calloused and Fair​
BUILD: Athletic, Nimble and Gentle​
NOTABLE FEATURES: ​
—> Eyes shine in the dark​
—> Tribal Clan Tattoo on his back, moving over his right shoulder blade.​
APPAREL: ​
—> White Petticoat w, Trauma plates sewn in on his elbows, forearms, around his collar (guarding his neck ), across his chest and down the sides of his torso. ( Think thin durasteel plates - they are light but durable; not impervious )​
—> Black longed sleeved Kimono top​
—> Black Hakama pants​
—> Black tactical boots with steel tips​
—> Green knit scarf​

APPEARANCE: ( As a Wolf )
​
HEIGHT: 3’ 5” ( 102 cm )​
FUR COLOR: Ivory​
WEIGHT: 120lbs ( 55kg )​
LENGTH: 4’ 5” ( 142 cm )​
TOP SPEED: 60 - 70 mph​
ABILITIES: ​
—> Force Howl​
—> Force Fear​
—> Force Speed​
—> Gurlanin Camouflage ​
​
FORCE ALIGNMENT: Bendu ( Neutral / Balanced )


STRENGTHS AND WEAKNESSES (Required: 2 Weaknesses Minimum) :
—> Melodic Melody:
As is case with the rest of his species, Quill has a naturally soothing voice that entices and captivates those around him and at times can make him extremely persuasive.
—> Nature's Child:
Since birth, Quill has had a unique connection and awareness to his surroundings. Aware of incoming weather, comfortable with any setting - Quill is most at home when in natural surroundings.
—> Shape Shifter ( Phasing ):
Generally always appearing in his humanoid form Quill has the ability to transform into a wolf. Much like his hair, he stands fully “clothed” in Ivory fur when in this form. Fast, agile and even telepathic with members of his own race, he is a true apex predator while in this form. While in Quills true form as a wolf, Quill is able to utilize his natural ability as a Gurlanin to mask his scent, vitals anything that could be traced by conventional methods.
—> Heightened Senses:
Thanks in large part to his mammalian genetics Quill has heightened senses and physical stamina. ( Hearing, Vision, Touch, Taste, Smell )
—> Long Life:
Quills heritage allows him to live up to 700 standard years.
—> Genius of Effort:
Living under the shadow of his beloved Uncle and dearly departed mother, Quill has had to live under the scrutiny of his name sake. This caused him at an early age to be engaged in training in various fighting styles. Never feeling like he lived up to the expectations or feeling like he was a natural Quill grew into his own legacy proving to others it was not the name that made him good - but rather his fierce determination to basics, fundamentals and doing it correctly so that when the need would arise, he would be ready and when it came to it, he was worthy as being dubbed alpha.

—> Aloofness:
Despite having an extremely loyal, caring and steadfast core; Quills perceived distant, disinterested and calculating demeanor at times comes across misinterpreted by others.

—>Tunnel Vision:
Thoughtful, and albeit wise for his age, large amounts of finite details can often overwhelm the young Quill.
—> Modesty:
Despite being polymorphic and having the ability to Phase, and not that modesty is a bad thing - Quill is unable to Phase when armor is present. If done hastily the previous clothing worn by Quill is no longer usable.
—> Young Pup:
As is the case with most younglings, Quill is often at times headstrong, and willful. Verses leaning in on the wisdom of those around him, if he gets it in his mind his way is the best, it’s hard to knock him from his course.
