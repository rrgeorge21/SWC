SPECIAL ABILITIES, POWERS

Reincarnated Primarch: Hadrian contains the essence of the XIth Primarch, Arcturus Valkorian, granting him numerous abilites:

  • Astral Psionic Affinity: Can draw upon the psionic powers of the Astral Plane deep beneath the Warp Layer.
  • Enhanced Physical Abilities: Possesses superhuman strength, speed, durability, endurance, stamina, reactions and agility as well as a high resistance to to magic, possession, mind/body manipulation, reality warping, and existence erasure
  • Brilliant Mind: Has a high level of excellence for whether understanding tactics and logistics, calculating warp jump calculations in his head faster than computers, or planning a campaign. To many he would be considered the consummate general and was arguably one of the most imaginative strategist and tactician amongst the Primarchs and was skilled at thinking on his feet, able to adapt and change his plan as the situation demanded with greater ease.

Lord Inquisitor: Trained as a Primaris Psyker, he also picked up a mastery in numerous weapons skills as well as an effective knowledge base in various Inquisitorial doctrines.

Abilities

Astral Psionics:

  • Micro-Cellular Manipulation: Hadrian's primary power granted him telekinetic abilities enabling him to levitate and manipulate living beings and inanimate objects at will. He primarily channels her powers through concentration alone, but has on occasion used hand gestures in conjunction with telekinetic feats. His telekinetic aura is colored red and glows around his hands, body, and the objects he telekinetically moves and controls. He can also channel this energy into a sort of regenerative healing power.
    • Psionic Energy Manipulation: He can project energy blasts, streams, waves and bolts of his own psionic and telekinetic energy, allowing him to hit, push/pull or blow away his targets, potentially exerting enough force to destroy them, or create protective force fields. This energy manifests in any shape or form, outlining his telekinetic spheres of influence, as the energy is visible in a reddish color.
    • Tactical Telekinesis: He can telekinetically further enhance his physical strength, agility, speed and balance to combinine his telekinetic power with his physical force and acrobatics.
    • Levitation: He can use his telekinetic abilities on himself or others to simulate a limited approximation of flight.
  • Neuro-Electric Sensitivity: Through a neuro-electric interface, Hadrian can manipulate the minds of others, and telepathically experience their memories, thoughts, and emotions. This can be extended further for a touch based Psychometric ability as well as mental interfacing with technology.

Divination:

  • Precognition: Hadrian has an inflow of prescience that allow him to foretell the movements of his opponents with a degree of accuracy that is often mistaken for natural instincts and quickened reflexes. He uses this ability to predict the path of projectiles, the danger of making one action versus another in flight, or as a highly accurate form of Danger Sense that not only informs him of imminent danger but also can assist him in taking steps to avoid that unseen and unknown danger.
  • Scrier's Gaze: The diviner can see the battlefield clearly in his mind's eye, with the vantage point of a hunting hawk and the precision of a laser. The to and fro of armies and war machines is but the scurrying of ants beneath his omniscient gaze.
  • Perfect Timing: As soon as his consciousness pierces the veil of the Warp, a rush of images floods the diviner's mind, foretelling the actions of his enemies, down to the merest microsecond. Armed with this information, the diviner's allies can predict exactly when their foes will peer out from behind cover and expose themselves to a lethal head shot.
  • Prescience: The psyker can effortlessly predict the paths of bullets and swords. By focussing his Warp-sight even more closely, he can guide his allies' aim, bringing a swift and merciless death to their foes.

Talents

Shadow Tree:

  • Dominate Mind: Controls a weak mind for up to 30 seconds.
  • Void Tendrils: Summons Shadowy tendrils out of the ground, rooting up to 5 enemiss within 8 yards. Killing the tendril will cancel the effect.

Holy Tree:

  • Desperate Prayer: Instantly heals Tomlin for a good part (1/3) of his life force.

Discipline Tree:

  • Spectral Guise: Tomlin's shadow blurs into the darkness, leaving his true form behind. As a shadow, Tomlin is almost invisible, but can actually fight.

Specializations

These are the powers that a specialized Shadow Priest gets access to.

  • Mind Flay: Assault the target's mind with Shadow energy, causing Shadow damage over time and slowing their movement speed by half. Takes some time to cast.
  • Vampiric Touch: Causes decent Shadow damage on touch, which is transfered to Tomlin's mana pool.
  • Shadow Orbs: Generated by Mind Blast and Shadow Word: Death. Used to empower Shadow Spells.
  • Shadow Form: The power of a specialist in the Shadow spells, which lets him assume a Shadowform, increasing his Shadow damage, as well as reducing all damage to him. It also increases the flow of magic around himself. However, he cannot cast Holy type spells in this form.
  • Psychic Horror: Causes terrible fear on Tomlin's opponents, likely to cause them to drop their weapons.
  • Vampiric Embrace: Fills Tomlin with Shadow energy, causing him to absorb the damage caused by his Shadow type spells and heal himself.
  • Dispersion: Tomlin can disperse into pure shadowy energy, making himself mostly intangible to physical or magical attacks. However, he cannot attack in this state.

NOTABLE ITEMS

Absolution Regalia: An armour blessed by the gods, complete with hood. It enhances magical capacity and allows Tomlin to cast spells with ease, while receiving limited magical enhancement.
Darkstaff of Annihilation: A powerful, Shadow-infused staff, which adds a spare mana pool for Tomlin to draw from when he casts spells.

WEAKNESSES

Magical Addiction: Since the destruction of the Sunwell at the hands of Arthas and the Scourge, the blood elves have been forced to deal with the sudden relapse of their addiction to arcane energy. A constant curse since the original Well of Eternity was destroyed, the addiction has become more powerful than ever. Blood elves spend their waking days struggling with their weakness, seeking either to sate it by siphoning magical energy from their surroundings or to resist the urge to feed.
Due to their addiction, a blood elf must spend one hour each morning in meditation, resisting any distractions from their withdrawal. Blood elves have learned to slake their thirst through the absorption of fel energies. If the blood elf partakes of demon’s blood, the magic addiction abates for a number of days.
While the blood elves do not abstain from draining arcane power directly, they are careful to keep their addictions under control, keeping the power their new abilities offer while maintaining self-discipline. Those who lose control over their addiction change into Wretched, and invariably fall into insanity and corruption.
