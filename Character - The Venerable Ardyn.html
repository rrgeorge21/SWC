​
EQUIPMENT​

Zakuulan Exarch Armour​
Made of various strong and well suited metals and alloys, Exarch armour was designed to work both as protection and conductor for the powers of an Exarch and to channel the energies of Sun Generators.​

It consists of a helmet with full battle interface and HUD, heat and night vision, a cuirass, shoulder guards with blade breakers, gauntlets, leg-guards, greaves and armoured boots, all connected with his body through intersection points across his skin to allow the armour to act as a second skin. Connected from the solar-plexus to the two shoulder guards is a power system to further help with reaction times.
Dominarch Artificer Armour​
Made by a famous master armourer, this armour was made of Duranium, Rhodium, Titanium, Frasium and Phrik in different alloys and combinations. Additionally it was gilded with a rare material called Auramite only found in a remote part of the late Eternal Empire.

The armour itself appears like an ancient lorica segmentata, with a dragon skull on the upper chest, the symbol of Valkorion and lights emitting energy from the interwebbed power source. The suit is made of a masterfully crafted chest piece, gauntlets and greaves, a cingulum and extended thigh plates.​
Shield of the Eternal Crusader​
Ecthelions shield is a large scutum, decorated with a dragons wings, lightning bolts and powered by a generator for the small deflector in it. The shield is made of the same materials as the Dominarchs armour and was forged by the same individual as well.​

Ecthelions lightsaberpike​
Powered by a light-blue coloured crystal with bifurcating cyclical-ignition pulse the weapon has a retractable hilt with four different length settings, ordinary lightsaber length, slightly extended hilt, truncheon length and pike length. The hilt is made of alloys of titanium, cortosis and auramite.​
Crusader gladius​
A short-sword with a design centered around stabbing and piercing and made of Titanium and Mullinine, this is a backup weapon designed for the Crusaders to serve in melee.​
Heavy blaster pistol​
A simple, unmodified heavy blaster pistol.​


​
STRENGTHS & WEAKNESSES​

Superhuman
Due to the extensive enhancements through cybernetics, implants and bio-engineering, Aiglos body is a few times more capable than the average person his stature, excluding any Force abilities. Reflexes, strength, speed and pain tolerance are vastly improved.		EMP vulnerability
The vast improvements in his body are based on artificial synapses and some, like his eyes or implants, are powered by the energy of the body turning it into forms of electricity. If shorted out, he is losing quite a bit of his potential. And he gets a very strong headache.
Venerably experienced
As a veteran of wars which are millennia ago, he has seen and experienced a lot more than a person can imagine, having spend the time to acquire a very large variety of skills.		PTSD
The death of Valkorion and the fall of Zakuul afterwards had left deep scars. Furthermore the torture he suffered at the hands of the Exchange demanded a tribute as well. If his mental shielding and conditioning is broken, it is easy to offset him.
Ancient combat practitioner
The combat form he uses is not known to the Galaxy, the Knights of Zakuul just a little note in the history books. It is a unique and efficient fighting style.		Expressionless, emotionless
Thanks to the Exchange, he is not able to show any facial expressions anymore. Furthermore his alterations, his losses and the huge amount of time which passed, dulled his emotions to a total lack of empathy.
Proven commander
As successful Sith hunter in the Great Conquest, a reckless Jedi killer in the centuries after, he had made his career under Arcann. Commanding is a key talent, not an inspirational, but as an efficient and reckless leader, taking the first spot in line himself.
