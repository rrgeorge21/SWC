[Personality]

Education, armour, self-defence, the clans, language, the Mand'alor. The Resol'nare is the most sacred aspect of a Mandalorian's life, therefore it was the hardest thing for him to do when Jagen Wren broke them. Jagen had lived the life of a warrior, of a soldier, for the first eighteen years of his life, attempting the reconstruction of the once-respected Clan Wren, until the beginning of the wars.

 

Jagen favours the Jedi philosophy of pacifism over the warmongering that the Clans achieved during the age of the One Sith and the Galactic Republic, sticking to it even though being declared a Dar'manda. After ascending to the rank of Knight, he became disillusioned with the Republic and finally exiled himself to Jedha after the attempted coup in order to distance himself from the wars and their bloodshed.

 

Over the last few years, he adopted alcoholism as a pass-time, preferring to waste away in the wastes of the desert moon as long as he had a cold drink in his hand. Finally returning to the Galaxy from his exile, Jagen wishes nothing more than to be left alone, although a sliver of adventure still happens to venture into his life from time to time...

[Appearance]

Passed his twenties, whispers are the only remains of the Jedi Master's prime. The years though, even in a desert, have been kind to his appearance. Through vigorous exercise and long daily treks, Jagen maintains his athletic figure although his height has shrunk a minuscule amount. His weight has also increased, giving him a slightly bulky appearance further exemplified by his robes.

 

Featuring grey-blue eyes since his birth, Jagen has light brown hair that has started to grey around the edges, common for all in their middle ages. His equally brown beard remains surprisingly untouched by age, the man has grown it out during his years-long exile but grooming it regularly to maintain what farce of professionalism he has left.

 

The Jedi Master can always be seen wearing the beige robes of his old Jedi Order along with the dark grey cloak that accompanies it. He also dons a utility belt where his lightsaber hangs and a scavenger's pack that is secured to his back. Goggles to protect his eyes from harm hang loosely around his neck.
